# Myrmec Hero & Value Proposition
**Role:** Marketing & Strategic Framing

## 1. The Hook
- **Headline:** "Orchestrate intelligence at scale."
- **Sub-headline:** "Decouple your business from infrastructure. Deploy, govern, and scale autonomous agent swarms without developer dependency."

## 2. Core Pillars (The 'What')
- **Pillar 1: Infrastructure Isolation.** DevOps manages the local Docker nodes and GPU clusters; Business users manage the tasks.
- **Pillar 2: Centralized Governance.** Admins define the "Guardrails" (Token limits, Model access, RBAC).
- **Pillar 3: Deterministic Sutras.** Turn non-deterministic AI into predictable business outcomes using structured agentic flows.
